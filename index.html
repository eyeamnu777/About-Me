About Me

Nerissa Leynes

Nerissa Leynes, 32, is a student from Code Fellows and the University of Maryland Global Campus, pursuing a degree in Software Development. Served eight years in Us Navy as a Logistic Specialist. Experienced in maintaining and replenishing supplies needed to support the staff and training personnel. Inventoried 403-line items with 100% location and inventory validity.

Nerissa also worked as a Government Purchase Cardholder, processing and procuring 115 requisitions valued at over $105,000 in commercial products to support command staff, students, and training events in the fiscal year 2019. Awarded and recognized for having a well-executed Purchase Card Program in FY19 and FY20.

She expertly led two sailors in the inventory of 21,000-line items, ensuring all receipts and issues were processed daily, and supported divisions and over 30 Repair Parts Petty Officers (RPPOs), issuing parts needed for maintenance and operational support.

Nerissa is also a motivated and dedicated student aspiring to be a Software Developer. Her goal is to be a part of tech industry, and hoping to be able to work in a company like Google.
